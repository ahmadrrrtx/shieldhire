How ShieldHire Works

1

Employer Posts Requirements

Sets minimum qualifications — years of experience, education level, and skill score. The setRequirements() transition writes these to the public ledger. Anyone can verify what the job requires.

🔗 Public Ledger
2

Candidate Applies Anonymously

Submits qualifications as ZK witnesses — private inputs that go into the circuit locally. The applyAnonymously() transition accepts secret_years, secret_education, and secret_skill. These values never leave the device.

🛡️ Shielded Ledger
3

ZK Proof Generated

The qualificationCheck() circuit runs locally via the Midnight Proof Server. It produces a ZK-SNARK proof that the candidate meets requirements — without revealing any witness values. Only the proof + boolean result go on-chain.

🔐 Zero-Knowledge
